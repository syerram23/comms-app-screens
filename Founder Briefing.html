NEW Magic V1 — The 30-Second App

The fastest path from idea to sent conversation. Three sidebar items. Voice-first builder. AI-generated results. Designed for someone who wants to send a conversation in under 30 seconds without learning an enterprise tool.

Why this exists: Enterprise buyers need to see the full platform. But the first-time experience should be magical — speak for 20 seconds, review what Comms built, hit send. Magic V1 is that experience. It's also the version we can demo in a sales call without overwhelming the buyer.

Product Flow

Builder (voice conversation with AI → auto-generated flow → edit → share/send) → Conversations (list + AI-generated results with summaries, action items, themes, individual responses) → People (participant directory with detail panels)

Key Features

Voice-first builder — The admin has a back-and-forth voice conversation with Comms AI. Comms asks clarifying questions ("What documents do you need? How many follow-ups?") and builds the full conversation flow. No forms, no blocks, no configuration screens.
AI-generated results — When participants complete, the Conversations screen shows a rich AI summary: natural language paragraph, stat cards (completion rate, avg time, validation rate), suggested action items (as checkboxes), key themes (metric bars), and individual response cards with expandable details.
Participant voice experience — The participant clicks a link, lands on a branded page, and has a voice conversation with an AI agent. No forms, no uploads (in Magic V1) — just a natural back-and-forth voice conversation. Dark immersive design with animated waveforms.
What's NOT in Magic V1: No templates library, no search/query, no settings, no integrations, no document upload in participant view. These are all in Enterprise. Magic V1 is intentionally stripped to prove the core loop works.

CORE Enterprise — Full Application

The production application with full sidebar navigation, campaigns, people directory, templates, natural language search, settings, and rich participant experiences (voice, video, documents, forms, e-signatures, presentations).

Why this exists: This is the product we sell to enterprise buyers. It handles the full lifecycle: build conversations (voice-first or from templates), send via email or link, track campaigns, search across all conversations with natural language, manage participants, and configure integrations. The participant experience supports every modality.

Product Flow

Sign Up → Onboarding (5 steps incl. template selection) → Home (conversational dashboard) → Builder (voice setup → edit → preview → send) → Campaigns (list → detail with AI summary → conversation replay) → People (directory → detail) → Search (NL query) → Templates → Settings

Key Features & Decisions

Voice-first builder (same as Magic, but with more options) — Template shortcuts, AI prompt, back-and-forth voice conversation. The enterprise version adds: templates library, advanced editor link, more delivery options.
Delivery: Email + Link only — No SMS, no phone calls. Branded email with configurable follow-ups (1-5 reminders) or a shareable link. This was a deliberate simplification from the original design which included SMS/phone.
Tour guide on first visit — 6-step interactive overlay highlighting Builder, Steps, AI Assist, Preview, Campaigns, Search. Stores completion in localStorage so it only shows once.
Friendlier labels for non-technical users — "Ask a question" instead of "Voice modality", "Collect a document" instead of "Document Upload", "Automatic checks" instead of "Validation rules". Designed for HR/People Ops buyers.
